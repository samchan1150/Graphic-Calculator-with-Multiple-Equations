How to Enter Equations

Enter your equations using standard mathematical syntax. Ensure that all parentheses are closed and that operators are correctly placed. For example:

  • Quadratic Equation: x^2 + 3x + 2
  • Trigonometric Function: sin(x)
  • Exponential Function: e^(x)

Use '^' for exponents, and functions like sin, cos, tan, log, and exp for trigonometric and exponential functions.
